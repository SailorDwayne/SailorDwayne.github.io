What Exactly is Crime Soup?

Crime Soup is a story-based point and click game following a detective cat named soup on his mission to solve a murder mystery! Solve puzzles, collect clues, investigate suspects, help your comrades, and drink milk from a saucer all under the watchful eye of that doggone Mayor and his chief of police. Who's this killer on the loose-- and how do you order your favorite coffee if all you can say is meow?
