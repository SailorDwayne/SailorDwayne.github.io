Who Are We?

Gædium is a small indie media company focused on bringing you content!
We focus mostly on game development, but we also animate, storywrite, and create artwork. You can visit our project pages on the left. Here at Gædium, we focus on individuality! Each of our creators is unique in their own way-- we work on our own projects, collaborate with other creators, and do what we love! For more information on individual creators, visit their portfolios to the left, listed bellow our project pages!
